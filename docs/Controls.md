# Controls
## Joystick

Button Number | Action
------------- | ------
1 | Shooter
2 | Climber
3 | Gear Drop
4 | Ball shift down
5 | Ball shift up
6/11 | right/left hand high goal
7/10 | right/left hand low goal

## PS3

Button Number | Action
------------- | ------
X | Climber
R2 | Shooter
L2 | Ball shift
L1 | Switch shooter
