# Controls
## Joystick

Button Number (Duncan) | Button Number (Kevin) | Action
------------- | ------
2 | 2 | Gate Down
3 | 3 | Gate Up
4 | 7 | Ball Shift Low
5 | 6 | Ball Shift High
7 | 4 | Flapper Open
6 | 5 | Flapper Closed
8 | 8 | Drive Forward
9 | 9 | Drive Backward

## XBOX

Button Number | Action
------------- | ------
RB | Climber Fast
LB | Climber Slow
Back | Climber Down
X | Shooter
A | Intake
