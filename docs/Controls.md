# Controls
## Joystick

Button Number | Action
------------- | ------
1 | Shooter
2 | Climber Up
3 | Gear Drop
4 | Ball Shift Down
5 | Ball Shift Up
6 | High Goal
7 | Low Goal
8 | Climber Down
10 | Intake On
11 | Intake Off

## PS3

Button Number | Action
------------- | ------
X | Climber
R2 | Shooter
L2 | Ball shift
L1 | Switch shooter
